Submissions


In order to submit an article, please follow these instructions:

  1. Ensure that you have followed the article guidelines carefully. Articles that do not follow these guidelines will not be reviewed.
  2. Join the Rutgers Research Review Sakai page. Note: You must have a Rutgers Net ID to join.
  3. Go to the Assignments tab of the Sakai page.
  4. Click on the available assignment (there should only be one).
  5. Copy and paste the text of your article in the text box. Attach any images used in your article as JPEG or PNG files. For any graphs or tables, please attach one Excel file for all graphs and/or tables and ensure that the title of the graph(s) and/or table(s) contain(s) the figure or table number and a description (e.g., "Figure 1: Comparison Between...", "Table 1: List of Materials...").
  6. Check the Honor Pledge box (the article will be checked for originality).
  7. Submit!

Your article will be reviewed by an editor who has experience in the discipline you are writing about. If necessary, you will be contacted with a reviewed and edited article. You should then make the appropriate changes based on the comments provided to you by the editor. When the article is ready, re-submit it to the same Sakai Assignment page that you submitted your initial draft.
