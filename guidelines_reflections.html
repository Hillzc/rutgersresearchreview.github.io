Reflections


If you are a Rutgers undergraduate student, Rutgers graduate student, or a Rutgers alumna/alumnus with previous research experience (academia or industry research), you are eligible to submit an article discussing your experience in a field of research. Discuss specific events from your experience in your field of research, your former and present points of view on research, and your advice to students who are interested in research.

    Example questions to focus on:

  • How have your experiences shaped your perception of research and/or future goals?
  • What important lessons have you learned from conducting research?
  • If you have experience in both, how do research in academia and industry differ?
  • How have your research experiences differed from your expectations?

Please ensure that the article contains 500 to 1,500 words. Structure the article in an appropriate manner based on your topic of discussion.

Submit your article to rutgersresearchreview@gmail.com. Your article will be reviewed by a Rutgers Research Review editor who is familiar with your general field of research. The comments will be sent to you for revision. After you submit the second draft with the recommended changes to the editor, the editor will make any remaining edits and publish the article on the website.
